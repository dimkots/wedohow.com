What we do
Brief intro text about services,
before explaining each one.
Corporate Training
Interested in recruiting engineers, coaching your tech leads or providing advanced web development training for your engineers? We have a vast variety of courses ranging from IT recruiting to advanced distributed systems.
Product Development
Have a product idea and don’t know how to implement it? Building on top of our combined 20+ years of experience, HOW’s team can design, implement, deploy and support your product idea, no matter how complicated it is. But no, we don’t fly to Mars!
Project Implementation
We can bring your web application to life and even take care of your Search Engine Optimization, so that your website ranks high on the popular search engines. We specialise in Ruby on Rails backend with React frontend, but we will work with you to determine the most appropriate technology stack for your needs.
Hiring
We will work with your HR and IT team to set up a robust interview and HR-to-candidate communication process so that you hire the best talent. Moreover, even the people you will choose not to hire will leave with the best impressions about your organisation and spread the word.
Interested in any of the above or not? Let’s talk.
